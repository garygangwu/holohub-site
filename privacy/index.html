Holo Player Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

HoloHub ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how Holo Player ("the App") handles information when you use the app on Apple Vision Pro.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Holo Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Account information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Video content or URLs you view

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs.

2. Local Video Playback

Videos you open from your device (MP4, MOV, etc.) are processed entirely on-device.

We do not upload, store, or transmit your files.

3. Web Video Playback

When you enter a website URL, the App loads the content directly from the requested website.

HoloHub does:

  • Not see the URL
  • Not proxy the video through our servers
  • Not track what you watch

Any data exchanged happens only between your device and the website you visit, according to that website's own privacy policy.

4. No User Accounts

Holo Player does not require sign-in and does not provide account functionality.

5. No External Data Sharing

We do not share any data with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertisers
  • Analytics companies

No data is sold or transferred.

6. Children's Privacy

Holo Player collects no personal data, and is safe for all ages.

Parents should be aware that the App can load any website the user enters.

7. Security

All network requests are established and secured by Apple's visionOS networking stack using standard HTTPS/TLS.

No additional security-sensitive processing occurs.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact:

Email: Contact Support
